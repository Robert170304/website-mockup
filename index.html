The Current Addiction Recovery Processes Don’t Always Work

There’s no program out there that’s:

  • Affordable
  • Non-religious
  • Completely judgment free

Which is exactly why we’ve created Can We Help Now.

What helped us get over our addictions was understanding the science behind why we get addicted, how our brains get affected, and how we can rewire our brains to stop repeating the addiction cycle.

Even though some addiction programs want you to “admit” that you don’t have power, the reality is that:

You are not Powerless

Yes, at times you might feel out of control, filled with regret, or like you want one thing or do another, but that doesn’t mean you are powerless.

It just means you don’t have the right tools to deal with what you’re facing right now.

It’s like trying to build a house when all you have is a guitar, a drum set, and a piano.

Sure, they sound beautiful and are great for building a band, but for building a house? Not so much.

But once you understand why your current behaviors and actions happen, have the tools to alter these actions and behaviors, and know how to use the tools and checklists to improve these behaviors... Your life will start to look very different.

And you don’t even have to leave your house to get these tools!

Overcome Addiction From the Privacy of Your Own Home

We know first hand that there’s a stigma that comes with addiction.

There shouldn’t be. But there is.

And some people can have negative impacts on your journey to sobriety and even make you question what you are doing.

That’s why we’ve created this program to be completely anonymous and self-paced.

You don’t have to tell your boss, your coworkers, or your friends you’re doing this. You can just get started and tell them when you’re ready - if ever!

Your Roadmap to Getting Clean

It doesn’t matter whether it’s alcohol, drugs, pornography, or something completely different. They all have roots in the same chemical and physical processes in the brain.

And in this program we’ll show you how to help our brains dismantle these brain pathways that don’t serve us and how to create new, healthy pathways to improve your mental health, physical health, and your relationships without falling into those annoying shame & guilt cycles.

Here’s what the process looks like:

Module One: Understanding Addictions and Addictive Behaviors

In this module you’ll learn exactly why addictions happen, get a concrete way of determining if you have an addiction, and get a better understanding of the process towards recovery.

Module Two: Understanding the Effects of Addictions and Addictive Behaviors

After completing this module you will understand the tricks, biases, and habits our brains use to trick us into repeating our addictive patterns. You’ll understand why those familiar shame and guilt cycles happen and how to break out of them.

Module Three: Tools To Kick The Habit

In this module you will learn the exact tools you need to kick the habit for good.

Meet Your Instructors

Mark Alton

I was an expert at losing things because of my addictions. I lost my wife and I’ve experienced discrimination at work because of them. I’ve been through two treatment centers and have attended AA and SMART recovery meetings. I’ve been sober and I’ve relapsed, but it wasn’t until I understood what was scientifically happening in my brain that I finally felt in control and like I had a clear path to breaking free of my addiction.

As I went through my recovery process I realized I wasn’t a huge fan of the “give away control” and “admit you’re a terrible person” aspects of the recovery programs I was in... but when I looked for something different I saw there was no holistic program out there that was effective, based in science, and didn’t cost tens of thousands of dollars.

That’s when I knew that when I figured this out... I would create a program to help others tame their own addictions once and for all

Matthew Martin

My addictions took a different toll on my life. I was addicted before I met my wife and had kids, but when I was addicted I never noticed any effects on my family, my parents, or people I was friends with. Only after I got clean did I realize I hadn’t talked to my brother or sister in years.

I had memory lapses that lasted for multiple days. I’ve woken up in different countries without realizing it, and I’ve lost track of time multiple times when I was addicted.

But I knew I had to stop when I saw a video of myself and knew I never wanted my future kids to see me like that. I got clean ever since, but after meeting Mark I realized that everyone has a different journey to getting clean and I wanted to make this journey easier for other people.

FAQs

How do I know this course will work for me?

There is no guarantee that any type of program or rehab is right for everyone. For us, understanding the science really hit home and allowed us to gain power over our addictions. We believe you can do the same and gain power over your addictions with a program that doesn't judge you for the choices you have made in the past.

Is there a money back guarantee?

We’ve priced this program to be affordable for everyone, no matter what your addiction is. It’s less than 1/100th of the cost of private rehab clinics, and 1/10th of the cost of many other programs so we are not offering a money-back guarantee. This is your time to make a commitment to yourself, your family, and your health and we want you to make that commitment as soon as you join the program.

How long will this course take to complete?

We didn’t design this course to be something you take once and never come back to. We created it as a resource you can come back to over and over again.

Is your course certified by addictions specialists?

No, this course is about tools and methods that worked for us and other people in addiction. We built this course based on our experiences, to strengthen our recovery, and to help you go through the same process.

Will this course help me get sober straight and help me when I have cravings?

We can’t make any promises, but if you put in the work, time and effort, our goal is to provide you with the tools, worksheets, and support that you can use whenever you are triggered or have cravings.

Will people know I’m taking this program?

The benefit of this course is that you can start your recovery in obscurity and bring it to light when you are individually strong enough to do that within your own narrative.
